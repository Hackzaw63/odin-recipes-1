Garlic Butter Pork Chop

These Garlic Butter Pork Chops will create the greatest mouth explosion you've had since having pop rocks as a kid.

Who doesn't love the fatty, juiciness of Pork chops? These pork chops bring that fat and add a great kick of garlic and butter to blast your taste buds into next week! Let's get cracking on this wonderful dish!

Ingredients

  • 4 x Bone-in Pork chops
  • Coarse salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 tablespoon butter (divided)
  • 2 springs fresh thyme - (plus 1 tbsp fresh chopped thyme, stems removed)
  • 6 cloves garlic - minced

Let's Get Cooking

  1. SEASON: Generously season both sides of prok chops with salt and pepper
  2. COOK: Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the pan is hot, and the chops, 2 tablespoons butter, and 2-3 sprigs of fresh thyme to the skillet. Cook the pork chops for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the outside is golden and the internal temperature reads 145Â°F (or a little below) with a digital meat thermometer.
  3. Add the remaining butter, garlic, and thyme: Add the remaining butter, minced garlic, and chpoped thyme to the skillet. Use a spoon to drizzle the garlic butter sauce over the pork chops before flipping the pork chops over and spooning the garlic butter over the pork chops on the other side. Repeat 1-2 minutes
  4. SERVE: Immediatly remove pork chops from the skillet and serve with all your favorite sides. Enjoy!
