Sloppiest of Joes

Home

Created with love and care this wonderful homemade sloppy joe creates a mouthwatering feeling in your mouth even before they are done cooking.

Who doesn't love a good home cooked meal to take them back to their childhood. These sloppy joes are an elegant take on a classic meal to feed the whole family. This recipe is easy to make for larger groups or just the small family dinner. Give them a try while trying to make them your own style!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs extra lean ground beef
  • 1/2 onion (diced)
  • 1 green pepper (small diced)
  • 4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons worcestershire sauce
  • 3 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • fresh ground black pepper to taste
  • 8 hamburger buns for serving

Let's Get Cooking

  1. Add ground beef and onions to a large skillet set over medium high heat and cook until beef is browned.
  2. Add all remaining ingedients (except for the buns!) and cook for 20-25 minutes, or until beef is crumbly and liquid has formed a thick sauce.
  3. Serve on a toasted bun

NOTES (Always have to have notes ;)

The longer the mixture cooks, the thicker it will get. You want the sloppiest of joes to be thick enough to stand up on a wooden spoon so they don't ooze out of the hamburger bun.
